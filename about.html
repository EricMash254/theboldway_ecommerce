Esther Gicharu

Founder/CEO of The Boldway

I'm Esther Gicharu, the founder and CEO of The Boldway Collection, where we specialize in creating premium leather designer bags right here in Kenya. Our brand is all about showcasing the incredible craftsmanship of Kenyan artisans while supporting local communities and promoting sustainability. At The Boldway Collection, we offer a diverse range of meticulously crafted bags, from elegant handbags to versatile totes, all made from locally sourced materials. What sets us apart is our commitment to customization, allowing our clients to collaborate with us on bespoke designs that truly reflect their unique style. With a focus on innovation and a passion for ethical practices, I'm proud to lead The Boldway Collection towards a future defined by excellence, creativity, and social responsibility.

The Boldway

Origin of the company

The origins of The Boldway Company trace back to a fervent vision held by its founder, Esther Gicharu. Inspired by the rich heritage of Kenyan craftsmanship and a desire to showcase it on a global stage, Esther embarked on her entrepreneurial journey with a single purpose: to create a brand that seamlessly blends luxury, authenticity, and social responsibility. Drawing upon her deep-rooted connections within Kenya's artisanal community, Esther established The Boldway Company as a testament to the country's talent and ingenuity. From humble beginnings, the company flourished, fueled by Esther's unwavering commitment to quality and sustainability. Today, The Boldway Company stands as a beacon of African luxury, celebrating the beauty of handcrafted leather goods while uplifting local communities and championing ethical practices.

Services

Our Services

Consultation

At The Boldway Company, it all begins with a personalized consultation, where clients express their preferences and ideas for their custom leather bag, allowing us to gather essential information to understand their vision.

Design Conceptualization

After the consultation, our designers collaborate closely with clients to craft a unique design that captures their style and needs, whether it's a handbag, tote, or backpack.

Material Selection

Once the design is finalized, clients choose from locally sourced premium materials, ensuring top-notch quality and durability, including luxurious leathers and durable hardware.

Craftsmanship

With design and materials confirmed, our skilled artisans meticulously craft the leather bag, ensuring precision and attention to detail in every step, from cutting to stitching and assembly.

Quality Assurance

Quality is paramount at The Boldway. Each custom leather bag undergoes rigorous quality control checks to meet our high standards. We meticulously inspect every aspect, from stitching to finishing touches, ensuring impeccable craftsmanship and durability.

Delivery

After passing our quality assurance process, custom leather bags are carefully packaged for delivery. Clients select their preferred shipping method, and we guarantee timely and secure delivery to their doorstep.

Mission

To empower individuals to embrace their unique boldness, inspire fearless exploration and redefine the intersection of style and adventure through crafting high quality bags.

Vision

To be a fashion brand that is a catalyst for change, fostering like minded individuals who dare to challenge conventions and forge their own path.
